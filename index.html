    Our story

    Our concept

    My city garden aims to help urban people escape from the city, find themself again in this chaotic world, and reconnect with nature, take a breath in our eco-friendly farm in the center of Paris and Lyon. You can plant and cultivate your own vegetables, fruits and flowers, even for those who have not much time, moreover, we give you the opportunity to sell your cultivated products in our marketplace. This city farm is not only helping adults but also children and young adults to understand the farming and cultivation process, it educates future generations to be responsible and eco-friendly consumers. Last but not least, by farming you increase your mental and physical health, which causes less depression and burnout.

    Our experts

    Experts are disposal at anytime to help you with gardening or questions concerning this one. My city garden experts have specifique training in gardening field and they can respond to you in every domain, such as questions concerning what type of flowers to plant in specific area or which fruits and vegetables are the best to plant in winter, etc. You can directly rech out our experts on our chatbot.

    Protect our environement

    Our building is composed of solar panels and we reuse water to create green energy with it, nothing is wasted in our company. We hope that our project will inspire more people to have an eco-friendly lifestyle and stop massive farming, deforestation and reduce pollution, which is now causing serious consequences for every being on this planet.
